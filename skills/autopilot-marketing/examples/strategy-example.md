## Content Themes (Rotation)

1. **Educational** (tips, how-tos, industry insights)
   - Example: "Top 5 ways to reduce member churn"
   - Example: "Why bilingual support matters for gyms"

2. **Social Proof** (testimonials, case studies, results)
   - Example: "How Fitness Plus increased retention by 40%"
   - Example: "@gymowner: 'MemberBook saved us 10 hours/week'"

3. **Product Features** (highlight key capabilities)
   - Example: "Automated billing + WhatsApp messaging = happy members"
   - Example: "Manage unlimited members, unlimited pricing tiers"

4. **Behind-the-Scenes** (team, culture, development)
   - Example: "Meet our customer success team"
   - Example: "What we shipped this week..."

5. **Industry News** (trending topics, research, analysis)
   - Example: "The rise of hybrid gym management"
   - Example: "2024 Fitness Industry Report released"

6. **Direct CTAs** (sign-ups, trials, special offers)
   - Example: "Launch your member portal in 5 minutes"
   - Example: "Free trial + onboarding for your gym"

## Platform-Specific Guidelines

### Instagram
- **Tone:** Casual, visual-first, emoji-rich
- **Length:** 100-150 characters
- **Hashtags:** 3-5 relevant hashtags
- **Emojis:** Use 1-2 relevant emojis
- **Best Time:** 9:00 AM
- **Example:**
  ```
  The #1 reason gyms switch to MemberBook? ⏰
  Automate billing, track members, launch WhatsApp notifications
  in under 5 minutes. No spreadsheets. 📊

  Try free: link in bio 🎯
  #GymManagement #MemberRetention #FitnessTech
  ```

### Facebook
- **Tone:** Conversational, storytelling, community-focused
- **Length:** 150-250 characters
- **Format:** Short story or question
- **Engagement:** Ask questions to drive comments
- **Best Time:** 9:05 AM
- **Example:**
  ```
  "Running a gym is hard. You've got equipment to maintain,
  members to manage, and spreadsheets... everywhere.

  What if billing was automatic? Member records were organized?
  Retention alerts came straight to WhatsApp?

  That's what MemberBook does. Thousands of gym owners are
  already here. Are you ready to join them?

  Try free, no card needed: [link]"
  ```

### LinkedIn
- **Tone:** Professional, thought leadership, industry insights
- **Length:** 200-300 characters
- **Format:** Professional insight or data
- **Audience:** Gym owners, managers, decision-makers
- **Best Time:** 9:10 AM
- **Example:**
  ```
  "77% of gym members cancel because they lose interest.

  The solution? Engagement. Automated reminders. Personal touch.

  Member management software that actually works for your business:
  ✓ Automated billing & payment tracking
  ✓ WhatsApp member notifications
  ✓ Retention analytics & insights
  ✓ Unlimited pricing plans

  Stop losing members to spreadsheet chaos. See how MemberBook
  can transform your gym operations: [link]

  #GymManagement #FitnessIndustry #Entrepreneurship"
  ```

### Twitter
- **Tone:** Punchy, conversational, trending-aware
- **Length:** < 280 characters
- **Format:** Short, quote-tweet friendly
- **Best Time:** 9:15 AM
- **Example:**
  ```
  "Your gym is losing ₹5L/month in preventable member churn.

  Automate your billing. Send WhatsApp reminders. Track retention.

  MemberBook does it all. Try free 👇 [link]
  #GymManagement"
  ```
