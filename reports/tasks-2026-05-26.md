# MemberBook Analytics Tasks - 2026-05-26

Source report: `reports/analytics-report-2026-05-26.md`
Period reviewed: 2026-04-28 to 2026-05-26
Compared against: `reports/analytics-report-2026-05-01.md`, `reports/analytics-report-2026-03-29.md`

## Summary

Overall health is good and improving. Sessions are up +27.1% (89 vs 70), page views jumped +273.7% (370 vs 99), average session duration recovered to 3m35s, and bounce rate fell from 79.5% to 69.7% (out of the critical band). Search visibility is strong: average position recovered from 10.5 to 6.8 and impressions rose to 1,825.

However, two problems carried over from the 2026-05-01 period remain unresolved and are now the top priorities:

1. **Search CTR is the broken link.** More impressions and better rankings are NOT converting to clicks. Overall CTR actually dropped from 1.6% to 1.3%. The gym revenue calculator page alone has 1,406 impressions (77% of all site impressions) at a 0.6% CTR — only 8 clicks. This is the single highest-leverage fix on the site.
2. **GA4 funnel events still report 0.** The 2026-05-01 task to add CTA/register/signup/org-created events was marked complete, but the verification subtask was skipped, and this report confirms all funnel events are still 0. Conversion optimization is impossible while blind.

Cloudflare Workers and D1 customer data were skipped again because Wrangler OAuth is expired and `CLOUDFLARE_ACCOUNT_ID` is unset — second consecutive period with no reliability or real-customer-count data.
