# MemberBook — Product Context

## Product Purpose

MemberBook is a membership management SaaS for small Indian businesses: gyms, libraries, and tuition centers. It replaces handwritten registers and spreadsheets with a single dashboard for members, subscription plans, payments, renewals, check-ins, and WhatsApp reminders. Free to start, India-focused.

## Register

mixed — public pages (homepage, landing) are **brand**, authenticated dashboard is **product**

## Users

Primary: gym owners, library managers, tuition center operators in India. 1–5 staff per business. Not tech-savvy. Use the app on mobile during the day, laptop in the evening. They care about getting paid and reducing no-shows. Secondary: gym/library staff managing daily check-ins and payments.

## Tone

Practical, warm, direct. Plain language. No startup jargon. The product should feel like a helpful back-office assistant, not a SaaS platform.

## Anti-References

- Generic SaaS cream (white + bright blue CTA, gradient hero, abstract blob art)
- Enterprise-dark (navy/gray/gold, data-heavy, intimidating)
- Anything that feels like it's for Silicon Valley startups

## Design Principles

1. Clarity over cleverness: every screen should be understandable in 3 seconds
2. Mobile-first: primary user checks the dashboard on their phone
3. India-native: INR currency, WhatsApp integration, regional context
4. Trust through simplicity: fewer features visible at once = less overwhelm

## Key Flows

1. Add member → assign plan → record first payment
2. Check dashboard for pending dues
3. Send WhatsApp renewal reminder from member profile
4. Record daily check-ins

## Color Strategy

Restrained (product), with Committed moments in the brand register. One primary accent (indigo) with amber as a warm secondary for financial/payment contexts.
